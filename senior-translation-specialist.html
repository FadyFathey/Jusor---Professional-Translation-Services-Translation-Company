Senior Translation Specialist

Join our team as a Senior Translation Specialist and contribute to delivering high-quality translation services that make a global impact.

Job Overview

We are looking for a Senior Translation Specialist with a strong background in legal and business translations. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience in managing translation projects, ensuring accuracy, and maintaining the highest standards of quality.

Responsibilities

  • Translate complex legal and business documents with high accuracy and cultural relevance.
  • Manage translation projects from start to finish, ensuring timely delivery.
  • Work closely with clients to understand their specific needs and requirements.
  • Proofread and edit translations to ensure consistency and quality.
  • Provide guidance and mentorship to junior translators.

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in professional translation.
  • Fluency in Arabic and English (additional languages are a plus).
  • Proven experience in legal and business translation.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to manage multiple projects.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Location

Dubai, UAE
